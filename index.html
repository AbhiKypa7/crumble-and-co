Our Story

A Bengaluru Bakery Born from Passion

Crumble & Co. was founded in 2013 by Meera Nair, a self-taught baker who turned her Sunday morning hobby into a beloved neighbourhood institution. What started as a small window shop in Indiranagar has grown into a full artisan bakery serving the entire city.

Every item is made from scratch using locally sourced ingredients — from the stone-milled flour to the farm-fresh eggs. We believe good bread takes time, which is why our sourdoughs ferment for 48 hours and our pastries are laminated by hand.

No artificial preservatives or flavourings
Locally sourced, seasonal ingredients
Fresh bakes every day from 7 AM
Custom cakes & catering for events
Vegan and gluten-free options available
